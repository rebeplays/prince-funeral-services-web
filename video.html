Cremation Package

Cremation Package

Our cremation package includes cremation with an urn and name sticker. For families wishing to view the cremation remains, we also provide amenities such as tarpaulins, tents, chairs, tables, water dispensers with mineral water, playing cards, a picture frame with a photo, flowers, white balloons for the interment, and a funeral car.

For Viewing
